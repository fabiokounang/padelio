About Padelio

Padelio is a lightweight helper for padel sessions. It is designed to make tournament setup easier, scoring faster, and player rotation more practical for clubs, community games, and friendly events.

What Padelio helps you do

Instead of manually organizing names, rounds, and score notes during a session, Padelio helps you create a tournament, choose the number of courts, set a target score, add players, and move through rounds with a live tournament structure. This keeps the session cleaner and easier to manage, especially when many players rotate in and out.

Who this app is for

Padelio is made for friendly organizers, club admins, community hosts, and padel groups who want a simple system for Americano-style sessions. It is especially useful when you want to keep everyone involved, avoid confusion, and maintain a fair flow across multiple rounds.

What is Americano in padel?

Americano is a social tournament format where players rotate partners and opponents across rounds. The goal is not only to win one match, but to collect as many points as possible over the whole session. This format is popular because it keeps the event active, social, and competitive at the same time.

How do points work in this app?

You choose a target score for each match, such as 16, 21, 24, or 32 points. When one team score is entered, the opposing team score is automatically completed based on the selected target. This helps keep matches short, consistent, and easy to track.

What is the difference between Normal and Mix Americano?

Normal Americano can be used without gender balancing. Mix Americano is designed for sessions where male and female player counts should stay balanced. This helps organizers create a more structured mixed format when needed.

Does it save my data?

Yes. Your tournaments and scores are stored locally in your browser on your device. This means you can reopen the app later on the same browser and continue where you left off, as long as your browser storage has not been cleared.

Can I use it on mobile?

Yes. Padelio is designed to work well on mobile so organizers can manage sessions courtside without needing a laptop. It can also be installed to the home screen as a Progressive Web App for quicker access.

How to install the app (PWA)

iPhone / iPad (Safari):
1) Open the website in Safari.
2) Tap the Share button.
3) Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
4) Rename it if needed, then tap Add.

Android (Chrome):
1) Open the website in Chrome.
2) Tap Install app if the prompt appears.
3) If it does not appear, tap the browser menu and choose Install app or Add to Home screen.
4) Confirm to finish installation.

Is this official tournament software?

No. Padelio is a community utility app built for friendly and social sessions. It is not an official federation system, not a certified competition platform, and not intended to replace formal event software used in professional tournaments.

Note: Padelio is designed to support community play and social tournament organization. For the best experience, use it on a modern browser and keep your device storage active if you want your tournament data to remain available.
