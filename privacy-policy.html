Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1) Introduction

Padelio is a lightweight community utility app created to help users organize friendly Americano padel sessions. This Privacy Policy explains what information is stored, how the app works on your device, and how third-party services such as advertising providers may interact with the website.

2) Information stored in the app

Padelio does not require account registration and does not ask users to create a personal profile in order to use the core tournament features. Tournament information such as tournament title, player names, scores, rounds, and leaderboard progress may be stored locally in your browser on your own device.

3) Local storage

Tournament data is stored in your browser storage so you can reopen the app later without losing progress on the same device and browser. This local data is not intended as cloud backup. If you clear browser data, reset your device, uninstall the app shortcut, or use a different browser, your stored tournaments may no longer be available.

4) Personal information

Padelio is designed to work without collecting direct personal identity information such as full legal name, password, billing information, or account credentials. Player names entered into tournaments are created by the user for session organization purposes and remain on the local device unless the user chooses to share them separately.

5) Advertising and third-party services

Padelio may use third-party services such as hosting providers, content delivery services, analytics tools, or Google AdSense to support the operation and maintenance of the website. These third parties may collect standard technical information such as browser type, device type, approximate location, referral data, cookies, or anonymous usage signals according to their own policies.

When advertising is shown, Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver and measure ads.

6) How data is used

Local tournament data is used only to provide the app experience on your device, including saving progress, showing round information, calculating scores, and displaying the leaderboard. Padelio does not sell user-entered tournament data and does not provide a user account system for storing private information on remote servers.

7) Data security

Because tournament data is primarily stored locally in your browser, you are responsible for managing access to your device and browser. No method of digital storage is perfectly secure, but Padelio is designed to minimize unnecessary collection of personal data by keeping the app simple and local-first where possible.

8) Children’s privacy

Padelio is a general utility website and is not specifically directed to children. Users should avoid entering sensitive personal information into tournament fields or other parts of the app.

9) Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect product changes, legal updates, or service changes. When updates are made, the “Last updated” date on this page will also be revised.

10) Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to report an issue related to the app, please contact the developer through the official Padelio contact channel or website contact page when available.

Reminder: If you switch devices or browsers, tournament data stored locally on the previous device may not transfer automatically.
